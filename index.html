This is Yousef Elrashidy

Young Egyptian Entrepreneur

Yousef Mostafa Elrashidy, also known as Yousef Elrashidy, He is the CEO of Elrashidy Media Group, was born on September 23, 2006 in Giza, Egypt. He began his career in the music industry as a musician and music director in 2018. In 2022, he released two instrumental EPs. Previously, Elrashidy was the founder of "Sada El Shaab El Masry" newspaper in 2019, but has since moved on from that role. He also worked as the President of Media Relations at Mashaweer Culture Forum and in public relations at Al-saaidyah Military High School. Elrashidy has experience in the production of cinematic, musical, and advertising works through his company, SkyBird Marketing, of which he was the CEO from July 17, 2021 until his departure. Manhom
